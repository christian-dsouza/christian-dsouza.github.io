Hobbies/Activities

Playing sports is one of the ways I try to maintain a healthy lifestyle. My competitive nature allows me to have a good time when playing sports, and motivates me to stay energized and active. My interest in fitness has also influenced me to go to the gym consistently.

My favourite sport is tennis! I have been playing tennis since I was 7 years old, and have enjoyed training and competing. To this day, I still closely follow the sport and have been fortunate enough to watch some professionals compete live! My favourite player is Roger Federer.
